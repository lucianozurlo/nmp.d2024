The PillTrack is a product that helps patients to keep compliant with their medications. Doctors and patients can view compliance rates in their online portals. It helps patients to keep track off their medications doses through text reminders and intuitive light indicators.

We were contacted by Athelas's engineering team to give shape to their idea, to make it attractive and modern yet discreet in an elder people's environment.

LED Indicator

The device has an LED indicator for each pill bottle, working between red and blue, showing which medication has been taken (blue) or not (red).

If the patient is ever late to take a dose, the PillTrack will help them by sending a reminder notification to their mobile phone.
